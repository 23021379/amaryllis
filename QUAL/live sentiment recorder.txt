### **1. The "Hyper-Local" News Radar (Google News API)**

**The Insight:**
Before a "Stop the Solar Farm" group forms, a local journalist writes an article.
*   *Headlines:* "Monster Farm Planned for Congresbury" vs "Green Energy Boost for North Somerset."
*   The **Comment Section** below these articles is where the *uncensored* sentiment lives.

**The Implementation:**
*   **Tool:** **SerpApi** (Google News Engine) or **NewsAPI.org**.
*   **Query Pattern:** `"{Village Name}" AND ("Solar" OR "Photovoltaic" OR "Renewable")`.
*   **Frequency:** Daily at 08:00.
*   **Extraction:**
    1.  Get the Article URL.
    2.  Use Python `newspaper3k` library to parse the body text.
    3.  **The "Vibe Check":** Feed the headline and first paragraph to an LLM (GPT-4o-mini).
    4.  **Classification:** `Positive` / `Neutral` / `Hostile`.

**Actionable Intel:**
If a local paper runs a "Hostile" story, the `nimby_coefficient` for that week spikes by 50%. The client must issue a "Myth-Buster" press release immediately.

### **2. The "Action Group" Detector (Change.org / GoFundMe)**

**The Insight:**
This is the **single most dangerous signal** for a live application.
If the opposition is organized enough to set up a Petition or a Fundraising page (to hire a barrister), your client is in trouble.
*   *Note:* These pages are indexed by Google immediately.

**The Implementation:**
*   **Tool:** **SerpApi** (Google Search).
*   **Query (The "Google Dork"):**
    *   `site:change.org "Congresbury" "Solar"`
    *   `site:gofundme.com "Stop" "Solar" "{Location}"`
    *   `site:justgiving.com "Legal Fund" "{Location}"`
*   **Logic:**
    *   If **Result Count > 0**: trigger **RED ALERT**.
    *   **Extract:** `Signature Count` or `Funds Raised`.
    *   **Velocity:** Track this daily. If signatures jump from 100 to 1,000 in 24 hours, the viral tipping point has been reached.

---

### **3. The "Facebook Loophole" (Public Groups)**

**The Insight:**
You cannot scrape *private* groups. But many "Action Groups" are *Public* to attract members. Google indexes these public posts.

**The Implementation:**
*   **Tool:** **SerpApi** (Google Search).
*   **Query:** `site:facebook.com/groups "{Village Name}" "Solar"`.
*   **The Trick:** Do not try to parse the Facebook HTML. Parse the **Search Snippet**.
    *   *Snippet:* "...join our fight against the ugly solar farm on Smith's Field..."
*   **Sentiment Analysis:** Even 150 characters of snippet text is enough for an LLM to identify the **Topic of Rage** (e.g., "Ugly", "Traffic", "Wildlife").

---

### **4. The "Search Volume" Proxy (Google Trends)**

**The Insight:**
If people are angry, they search.
If the search volume for "Solar Farm Health Risks" or "Solar Glare Epilepsy" spikes in the *specific region* of your client's proposal, you know exactly what the community is whispering about.

**The Implementation:**
*   **Tool:** `pytrends` (Unofficial API) or **Google Trends** (Official).
*   **Geo-Target:** Filter by Region (e.g., "North Somerset").
*   **Keywords:** "Solar Glare", "Lithium Battery Fire", "Property Prices Solar Farm".
*   **The Strategy:**
    *   If searches for "Battery Fire" spike: Tell the client to publish their **Fire Safety Plan** on the front page of their consultation website *tomorrow*.

### **Summary Blueprint**

1.  **Do NOT build a social media scraper.** It is a trap.
2.  **Build a "Search Listener"** using SerpApi.
